About Receiptly

Receiptly is a modern Android mobile application designed to help users digitise, organise, and track their pharmacy receipts. It offers features such as secure cloud-based storage, intelligent OCR scanning for easy data capture, prescription refill reminders, and a built-in pharmacy locator using Google Maps. Whether you're tracking your medication history or managing monthly expenses, Receiptly makes pharmacy-related finances clear and accessible.

Key Features

Smart Receipt Storage

Upload, scan, and organize your pharmacy receipts securely.

Prescription Tracking

Set reminders for refills and medication collection.

Pharmacy Finder

Locate nearby pharmacies directly from the app using integrated maps.
